Our Story

The Crosslink Group started with a straightforward observation: most small business owners are brilliant at what they do, and the marketing side rarely gets the attention it deserves. Not because they don't care. Because they're flat out doing the actual work.

We saw this across industries. A plumber in Penrith doing exceptional work, losing jobs to a competitor who posts on Facebook twice a week. An electrician in Brisbane, fully booked through word of mouth, until the referrals dried up one quiet winter.

The problem was never the quality of their work. It was visibility. And visibility has traditionally required either a big budget or a lot of time.

Crosslink Digital was built to change that. Using AI automation, we generate complete, professional marketing campaign packs in minutes and deliver them straight to the tradie's inbox. They post it. The phone rings.

No agency markups. No lock-in contracts. No learning curve. Just results.
